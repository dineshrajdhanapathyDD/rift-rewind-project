About Me

I'm a passionate Cloud and DevOps Engineer who believes that great infrastructure is built from the perfect blend of technical excellence, automation, and security-focused design.

My Journey

With over 2 years of experience in cloud infrastructure and DevOps, I've had the privilege of working with startups and established companies to build robust, scalable systems. My journey began with a curiosity about cloud technologies, and it has evolved into a deep passion for creating efficient, automated infrastructure solutions.

As an AWS Community Builder, I'm recognized by Amazon Web Services for my technical expertise and commitment to sharing knowledge with the cloud community. This recognition reflects my dedication to helping others learn AWS technologies and best practices.

I specialize in AWS cloud services, Kubernetes orchestration, infrastructure as code with Terraform, and building CI/CD pipelines that enable teams to deploy with confidence and speed.

What Drives Me

I'm motivated by the challenge of solving complex infrastructure problems and the satisfaction of seeing systems run smoothly at scale. Whether it's optimizing cloud costs, improving deployment reliability, or implementing security best practices, I approach each project with enthusiasm and attention to detail.

When I'm not working on infrastructure, you'll find me exploring new cloud technologies, contributing to open-source DevOps tools, or sharing knowledge with the technical community.
